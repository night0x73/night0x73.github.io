About me.

My name is Andy Szeto and I am a hobbyist programmer. I graduated from the UW with a BSc. in physics and a minor in math. I have a passion for sports (basketball, golf, football are my big 3), computer science, and most things quantitative.

I primarily develop in Java and Python, with some light C/C++ due to my enthusiasm for electronics and embedded systems. I enjoy using Java as a general purpose language; having experience building web applications using Spring Boot, Spring MVC, and Hibernate frameworks. Python has been with me since day one. It is my go-to tool for solving numerical and automation problems, but I have yet to understand its full potential. Life's path is full of deviations, but I always find myself back here, in the world of science and computers.
